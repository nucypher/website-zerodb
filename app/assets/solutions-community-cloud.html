Retail and Institutional Banking

Minimize Risk and Maximize Opportunity
Banks compete on both their retail and institutional business units. Their goal is to deliver the most advanced consumer products that give them an edge in the race to grow their business. However, new banking computation must be done with complete compliance and security. Most banks have secure computation on premise with Hadoop. However, evolving competition and costs constraints are pushing banks to the cloud. Some of the cloud problems ZeroDB solves for today’s banks include:

  • Information Technology Log Data management and analysis
  • Customer transaction data analysis
  • Fraud and risk modeling and computation

Hedge Fund & Quant Fund

The Pursuit of Alpha
Hedge funds and quant funds compete or die on the quality of their trading models. The most advanced funds already compete with the most advanced, big data tools. However, funds do not have the capacity to build their own End-to-End encryption functionality. As data exploits and bad-actor penetration grows in sophistication, the security of computational algorithms are valued at billions of dollars. Demanding End-to-End encryption from the big data cloud is the new standard and norm for the following problems:

  • Historical data modeling and back-testing
  • Enterprise data lakes for sliced data computation
