Your Key to Zero Data Breaches

Enterprise Grade Security and Data Protection for Big Data in the Cloud

Find out How

Cloud Storage and Computation.
On-premise Security.

Big Data is rapidly becoming accepted as the future platform for data analytics and business intelligence. The natural home for Big Data is the Cloud, which delivers scalability, elasticity, and flexibility, without the overhead of on-premise infrastructure. But the largest barrier to adoption is security of both the platform and the Cloud.

ZeroDB enables enterprises to use the Cloud for storage and computation while keeping encryption keys on-premise.

Strong, Performant Encryption.
Your Last Line of Defense.

Organizations are increasingly concerned with the risk and cost of unending cyberattacks. Perimeter security plays an important role, but is insufficient on its own. The ultimate protection against attacks is encryption at the data layer. As malicious attacks have become more sophisticated, the ability to protect and regularly rotate encryption keys, while providing access to authorized users and partners, is critical to delivering against this mandate.

ZeroDB is built on peer-reviewed, proven encryption algorithms, such as AES256-GCM and ElGamal, guaranteeing best-in-class data protection.

Regulatory Compliance.
Data Protection.

For many organizations, data protection is a regulatory and compliance mandate. The Global Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Gramm-Leach-Biley Act (GLBA), Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS), and Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SarbOx), all require that organizations adequately protect their sensitive data.

ZeroDB prevents unauthorized, and potentially malicious, access to confidential data.
