Enterprise Grade Security.
For Big Data in the Cloud.

Big Data is rapidly becoming accepted as the future platform for data analytics and business intelligence. The natural home for Big Data is the Cloud, delivering the scalability, elasticity and flexibility, without the overhead of owning significant infrastructure. Bu the largest barrier to adoption is security of both the platform and the Cloud.

ZeroDB delivers enterprise grade security and data protection for Big Data and the Cloud.

Strong, Performant Encryption.
Your Last Line of Defense.

Organizations are increasingly concerned with the potential risk and cost of seemingly inevitable security breaches. Perimeter security plays an important role, but alone is insufficient. The ultimate protection against attacks is encryption at the data layer. However, as malicious attacks have become more sophisticated, the ability to protect and regularly rotate encryption keys, while providing access to authorized users and partners, is critical to delivering against this mandate.

ZeroDB is built on peer-reviewed, proven encryption algorithms, such as AES256-GCM, guaranteeing cutting-edge security and best-in-class data protection.

Regulatory Compliant.
Data Privacy and Protection.

For many organizations, data privacy is a compliance issue. Regulations such as HIPAA, Sarbanes-Oxley, PCI DSS, the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act and EU Data Protection directives all require that organizations protect their data-at-rest and provide defenses against threats. This will be further compounded by the General Data Protection Regulation (GPDR) that was ratified and will become law in 2018.

ZeroDB prevents unauthorized, and potentially malicious, access to confidential data.
