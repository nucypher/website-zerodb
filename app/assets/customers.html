Leading cloud data security for the most security-critical industries

ZeroDB helps enterprises around the world benefit from cloud adoption, while ensuring that critical information is protected and meets requirements for security, privacy, and compliance.

SaaS Providers

ZeroDB enables software-as-a-service companies to give their customers enhanced control over and protection of their private data. ZeroDB lowers the liability for SaaS companies while enabling them to access customer data on an as needed basis.

Cloud Service Providers

ZeroDB drives cloud adoption for all types of users by enabling cloud service providers to ensure the end-user retains full control over their data without exposing themselves to exess risk of data breaches. By giving cloud providers a way to ensure data is always encrypted - even when in use - customers never need to worry about their cloud provider being compromised.

Financial Services

ZeroDB enables the financial services industry to benefit from cloud adoption, while protecting sensitive information and meeting compliance requirements, including PCI and GLBA. ZeroDB is used by leading companies in banking, investment services, credit services, insurance, and financial technology.

Media & Telecom

Telecommunication, cable, and media companies increasingly use the cloud to connect to customers and make their businesses more agile. But managing customer and business data across multiple countries raises privacy and compliance risks. ZeroDB works with these companies to enable the cloud while assuring control over sensitive data.

Governments

Government agencies around the world are moving to the cloud to streamline operations, improve accessibility, and reduce costs. But they also have legal obligations to protect private data and other sensitive information. ZeroDB works with governments globally to enable cloud adoption without losing control over critical data.

Healthcare

The cloud offers great benefits to the healthcare industry – improved accessibility and information sharing, reduced costs – but it also raises new privacy risks and HIPAA compliance issues. ZeroDB allows healthcare organizations to safely adopt the cloud, while maintaining direct control over private health information.
